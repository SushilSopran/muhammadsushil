About

Sushil.

Muhammad Sushil is a brand and founder, also known as Sushil, acting as an indie developer with more than 10 years experience. Currently, he is more focused on the scope of UX design. He considered himself a business designer, leveraging his technical skills to create practical, business-oriented solutions.


During childhood, he never tired of walking 20 minutes from home to his go-to cyber cafe just to learn about computing, using his school meal money. Sometimes, strange dogs would disturb his journey. When his parents found out his daily evening routine, they bought him a used Windows PC. The first thing he did was to experiment with C# language. From then on, he developed a love for the technical aspects of computers. During his teenage years, he discovered that building a website with multiple functions was the coolest thing.


Today, he and his team globally freelance, helping universal startups implement digital ideas into business realities. He still works on backend development, even though he spends more time on UX design. He is always ready to help anyone who wants to scale up their business with his uniquely talented approach.
