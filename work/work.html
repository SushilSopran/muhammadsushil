NEXT ACHIEVEMENT

Games Live


This project started as a prototype for market testing purposes. After a year, it evolved significantly, incorporating numerous features to become a complete product. The application underwent a 9-month trial phase to gauge response. Following this phase, the project scaled up to a larger scope. This project allows users to easily purchase video games via their smartphone and automatically connect to their desktop. While playing on their console, they can also watch live through the app. This project had limitations, one of which is that users cannot play directly on the phone. The main core of this project is that users can watch their video gameplay live on their phone, record it in high definition on-screen, and share it on social media while streaming.
